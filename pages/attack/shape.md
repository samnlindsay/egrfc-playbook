# 3-3-2 pattern

![alt text](/assets/images/332.png)
- Backs play to an edge (e.g. from set piece)
- Forwards form pods:
    - 3 near the ruck
    - 3 further infield
    - 2 on the far edge
- Each pod is directed by a “Captain of Play” (COP)
