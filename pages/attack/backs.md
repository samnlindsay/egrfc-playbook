{: .no_toc }

## Table of Contents
{: .no_toc .text-delta }

- TOC
{:toc}

Backs moves are grouped into 3 main categories, based on the position of the "playmaker" (the key player choosing the crucial pass):
- **10** - UK countries
- **12** - European countries
- **13** - Rest of the world

--- 

# Playmaker at **10**

## 🏴󠁧󠁢󠁳󠁣󠁴󠁿 Scotland &nbsp; <span class="badge-renamed">RENAMED</span>
_(formerly "**Box 1**")_

- 12 runs an out-to-in line
- Blind side winger sits behind 10 before running wide, taking the ball behind 12
- 10 picks the pass to put one of them through a gap

# Playmaker at **12**

## 🇮🇹 Italy &nbsp; <span class="badge-renamed">RENAMED</span>
_(formerly "**Box 2**")_

- 13 runs an out-to-in line; winger runs behind 12 to 13
- 12 picks the pass to put one of them through a gap

## 🇫🇷 France

- 10 sits deep as 12 and 13 run through, with 12 taking the ball
- 10 and the blind side winger track wide behind 12 and 13
- 12 pulls the ball back to 10 who straightens outside 13
- 15 runs out-to-in and 10 pulls the ball behind him to the blind side winger

# Playmaker at **13**

## 🇦🇷 Argentina &nbsp; <span class="badge-new">NEW</span>

- 13 pops it back inside to 12 or...
- 13 pulls it back inside to the approaching blind side winger cutting across
